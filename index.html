FAQ
COMMON
QUESTIONS
What is the One Percent Nation?
+
The One Percent Nation is a personal and professional development community built on one principle: get one percent better every day. We offer courses, coaching programs, live events, and a private member community to help individuals and teams break through limitations and build toward their potential.
What programs do you offer?
+
We offer three flagship programs — The Mindset Reset (for breaking internal barriers), Purpose Pathways (for aligning purpose with execution), and Leadership Ascension (for leading with confidence). These are available as workshops, courses, and cohort experiences. We also offer the I Can't book and a free Custom Goal Planner tool.
How do I access the Member Portal?
+
The Member Portal gives you access to the full course library, community, resource downloads, live event replays, and your personal progress dashboard. Click "Member Login" in the top navigation to sign in or create an account. Members can sign in with email or Google.
Are programs available for teams or organizations?
+
Absolutely. All three impact programs are designed to scale — from individual contributors to full leadership teams. We've delivered The Mindset Reset and Purpose Pathways to organizations looking to solve disengagement, misalignment, and leadership gaps. Reach out to us directly to discuss a custom engagement.
What's included in the book "I Can't"?
+
I Can't is a practical guide to dismantling the beliefs that hold you back. It walks you through self-discovery, mindset reprogramming exercises, and actionable frameworks you can apply immediately. It's currently available on Amazon for $19.99 with a 30-day money-back guarantee.
How do the live webinars work?
+
Live webinars are hosted regularly and cover core framework deep-dives, Q&A sessions, and accountability check-ins. All registered attendees receive a replay link. Members in the portal get access to the full replay archive. Reserve your spot on the webinar page to receive session details and reminders.
